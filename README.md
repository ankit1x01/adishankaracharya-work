## Core Philosophy Summary

**Advaita Vedanta** - Non-dualism

**Central Teaching**:

> "Brahma Satyam Jagat Mithya, Jivo Brahmaiva Na Aparah"
> _Brahman alone is real, the world is illusory, the individual soul is not different from Brahman_

**Four Mahavakyas** (Great Sentences):

1. "Prajnanam Brahma" - Consciousness is Brahman (Aitareya)
2. "Tat Tvam Asi" - That Thou Art (Chandogya)
3. "Ayam Atma Brahma" - This Self is Brahman (Mandukya)
4. "Aham Brahmasmi" - I am Brahman (Brihadaranyaka)
