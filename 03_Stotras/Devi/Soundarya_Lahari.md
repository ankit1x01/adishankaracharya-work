# Soundarya Lahari (सौन्दर्यलहरी)

**Meaning**: "Waves of Beauty"
**Length**: 103 verses
**Deity**: Goddess Parvati/Tripurasundari

---

## Structure

### Part 1: Ananda Lahari (verses 1-41)

Mystical and philosophical

### Part 2: Soundarya Lahari (verses 42-103)

Descriptive beauty

---

## Overview

Combines tantric philosophy with devotion. Considered a masterpiece of Sanskrit poetry.
