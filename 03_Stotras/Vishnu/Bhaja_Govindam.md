# Bhaja Govindam (भजगोविन्दम्)

**Also known as**: Moha Mudgara ("Hammer to Delusion")
**Length**: 31 verses (14 by Shankara + 14 by disciples + 3 additional)

---

## Overview

- Warning against attachment to worldly life
- Call to devotion and self-knowledge
- Famous refrain: "Bhaja Govindam, Bhaja Govindam, Govindam Bhaja Moodha Mate"
