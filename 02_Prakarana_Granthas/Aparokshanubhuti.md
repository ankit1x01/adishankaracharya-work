# Aparokshanubhuti (अपरोक्षानुभूति)

**Meaning**: "Self-Realization"
**Length**: 144 verses
**Focus**: Direct experience of the Self

---

## Content

- 15 steps to self-realization
- Practices for purification of mind
- Meditation techniques
- Direct realization vs. intellectual understanding

---

## 15 Steps

1. Yama (ethical restraints)
2. Niyama (observances)
3. Tyaga (renunciation)
4. Mauna (silence)
5. Desha (place for practice)
6. ...up to Samadhi (absorption)
