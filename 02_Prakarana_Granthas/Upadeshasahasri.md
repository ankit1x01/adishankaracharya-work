# Upadeshasahasri (उपदेशसाहस्री)

**Meaning**: "A Thousand Teachings"
**Format**: Prose (Gadya) and Verse (Padya) sections
**Authenticity**: Universally accepted as authentic

---

## Structure

- 18 chapters in verse (Padya)
- 3 chapters in prose (Gadya)

---

## Key Teachings

- Method of self-inquiry (Atma-vichara)
- Distinction between Self and non-Self
- Prerequisites for spiritual knowledge
- The nature of ignorance (Avidya) and liberation (Moksha)
