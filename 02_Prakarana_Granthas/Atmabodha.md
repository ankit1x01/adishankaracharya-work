# Atmabodha (आत्मबोध)

**Meaning**: "Self-Knowledge"
**Length**: 68 verses
**Audience**: Beginners in Vedanta

---

## Content

- Introduction to Self-knowledge
- Methods to discriminate Self from non-Self
- Nature of ignorance and its removal
- Analogies to explain non-duality

---

## Famous Analogies

- Space in pot vs. infinite space
- Wave and ocean
- Snake and rope
